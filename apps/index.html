InstructIT POC

Client-instruction confirmation tools for legal matters with binding effect — hash-chained, audit-friendly, browser-local. Two proofs of concept.
For criminal counsel

Bail Cheat Sheet

Counsel-facing reference for a bail hearing — for rusty counsel, public-defender training, or duty counsel under pressure. Not a client tool.

Differentiator: jurisdiction-aware. Pick the courthouse and it filters to the local bail program (Toronto Bail Program, JHS Peel/Brampton, BC Bail Supervision, etc.). Walks counsel through onus, three grounds for detention, and the Antic ladder.

  • s. 515(6) reverse-onus categories with section anchors
  • Antic ladder (rungs 1–7) with counsel positions per rung
  • Local bail programs (ON + BC) by courthouse
  • Three grounds for detention (St-Cloud-aware)

For BC family lawyers

Family Law Settlement

Pre-execution confirmation for BC separation agreements. 14-node decision tree anchored on the Family Law Act and the Brandsema / Miglin / Hartshorne framework.

Differentiator: opt-in, browser-local affect monitor (face-api.js) that watches for elevated stress at signing-critical nodes (FLA s. 4 voluntariness, final signing). Intercepts Agree clicks and records contemporaneous behavioural evidence in the audit log.

  • 14 confirmation nodes
  • SHA-256 hash-chained log
  • Optional camera affect monitor (privacy: never leaves the device)
  • Companion JSON Schema: 48 properties, 70 markers

For Canadian criminal defence

Criminal Guilty Plea

Pre-plea confirmation aligned with Criminal Code s. 606 plea-inquiry duties and defence counsel obligations. 11 confirmation nodes from charge-understanding through final plea instruction.

Differentiator: per-node citations to leading authority (Adgey, T (R.), Hanemaayer, Anthony-Cook, Wong, T (N.)) and law-society / criminal-bar guidance (LSBC, LSO, CBA Criminal Justice Section, CLA), surfaced inline in the decision tree.

  • 11 confirmation nodes
  • SHA-256 hash-chained log
  • Authorities + bar guidance loaded from data/ (serve over HTTP)
  • Companion JSON Schema: 16 required top-level fields, 39 markers
